What if it’s raining?

The tour will be delayed for a certin amount of time. If the weather conditions are not improved by then you will be given the choice to reschedule your tour or get a refund.

What should I wear?

You should go through the weather forecast and dress accordigly.

What if I am late?

Our tours start on time. When you book a tour, you will receive an email confirmation from us with the meeting details for that tour. If you are running out late to your tour, please communicate with us immediately at XiulanMa@example.com or call us at 12 345 678 910 and we will do our best to catch you up with the group (though at this point we cannot guarantee you’ll be able to meet up with the group)
