Far Far Away Tourism

We are a sustainable touring company providing environmentally sustainable tours in and around the Wellington region, using carbon neutral transport covering the natural history of the region, organic food and beverage outlets, Māori culture and history, as well as Wellington’s significant sites.

Far Far Away Tourism’s mission and passion are to host tourists from the world over, to share all that Wellington region, this amazing region, has to offer in life-altering experiences. Our success is measured by visitors who choose to return again and again with us, and who encourage their friends and family to experience the country for themselves.
